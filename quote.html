Favourite Quote

Words That Inspire Me Every Day

“ The expert in anything was once a beginner. Keep learning, keep building, keep going. ”

— Anonymous

“ Code is like humour. When you have to explain it, it is bad. ”

— Cory House

Why These Quotes?

As a beginner in Computer Engineering, I face new challenges every day. These quotes remind me that every expert started from zero. Whether it is HTML, CSS, or C++, every skill is built line by line, step by step.


I keep these words in mind whenever I feel stuck on a problem. They motivate me to keep practising and never give up on learning.
